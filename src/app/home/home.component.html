Laylat-al-Qadr 23 ramdhan 1444

The Night of Power (Arabic: لیلة القدر, romanized: Laylat al-Qadr), also rendered as the Night of Destiny, Night of Decree, or Night of Determination, also the Precious Night, the night when the Quran was first sent down from Heaven to the world and also the night when the first verses of the Quran were revealed to the prophet Muhammad SAWW; it is described to be better than a thousand months of worshipping.

YA ALI AS
